# Smart Cards

Confluence renders bare URLs as **smart cards** in three flavors:

- **inline card** --- chip inside a paragraph
- **block card** --- single-row card on its own line
- **embed card** --- full preview (videos, sketches, dashboards, ...)

adflux preserves all three losslessly.
